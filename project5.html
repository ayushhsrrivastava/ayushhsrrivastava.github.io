01. Strategy & Market Selection

To meet the surging demand for rapid, "minutes" delivery services, the organization identified high-revenue pockets across Bangalore for immediate fulfillment expansion. My role was to transition these identified zones from empty shells to fully functional "Darkstores."

This was a high-intensity transition project, moving from digital project management to physical site development. I was responsible for the end-to-end setup of five concurrent locations, ensuring each met the strict operational standards for rapid inventory processing.

Fig 1: Logistics & Infrastructure Layout Management

02. Infrastructure & Technical Setup

Physical Layout Mgmt

Orchestrated the physical floor plan management, including rack placement, optimized cable layouts for connectivity, and zone demarcations to maximize picker efficiency.

Compute & Connectivity

Spearheaded the technical compute setup at each site, ensuring hardware and network infrastructure were robust enough to handle high-frequency transaction data and inventory sync.

03. The Implementation Journey

Stepping into the SCM domain for the first time, I utilized a "learning-by-execution" approach to meet a strict 2-month deadline:

  • 01. Rapid Vendor Coordination: Managed multiple hardware and facility vendors simultaneously to ensure all five sites hit deployment milestones in parallel.
  • 02. Operational Readiness: Beyond the build-out, I coordinated the transition to the operations team, ensuring the sites were "minute-ready" for live customer orders.
  • 03. Resource Optimization: Delivered the full 5-site expansion within a ₹20 Lakh budget by meticulously auditing infrastructure material costs and labor timelines.
