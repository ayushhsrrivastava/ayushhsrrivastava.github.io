01. Project Brief

A prominent leader in the industrial electronics sector required a massive digital overhaul to support their complex wholesale and retail operations. The primary goal was to establish two distinct digital ecosystems—B2B and B2C—within a single, unified management infrastructure.

Over a 12-month period, I spearheaded the delivery through multiple strategic phases, focusing on deep backend logic to handle their unique pricing and dimensional calculations while providing high-end, tailored experiences for both consumer and professional clients.

Fig 1: Unified Architecture & API Orchestration Map

02. The Challenge

Complex Calculation Logic

Engineering a proprietary backend solution for product setup and cost calculations based on internal variables and industrial boxing requirements.

Dual-Channel Isolation

Achieving total separation between B2B and B2C storefront journeys while maintaining a single, streamlined platform for administrative operations.

03. The Implementation

We tackled the digital transformation by focusing on high-impact customizations across the entire user journey, paired with robust third-party system integrations.

  • 01. Customized Frontend: Complete overhaul of landing, product, and cart pages to meet specific professional and consumer UI/UX standards.
  • 02. API Orchestration: Integrated SendGrid for automated lifecycle communication and custom-built APIs for third-party logistics tracking.
  • 03. Phased Scalability: Deployed the project in scheduled increments over 12 months to ensure operational stability and zero downtime for the client.
