01. Project Brief

A prominent leader in the U.S. luxury jewelry and customization market required a "Platinum" level project plan to redefine their digital presence. The goal was to build a comprehensive e-commerce platform that married high-end aesthetics with complex functional requirements.

I spearheaded the end-to-end delivery of this one-year project, serving as the Project Coordinator. My mission was to manage the intricate balance between bespoke design requirements and highly customized backend architecture.

03. Cutting-Edge Integrations

To ensure a competitive edge in the luxury space, we integrated several advanced technology modules. These weren't just features, but core pillars of the digital shopping experience.

  • 01. AI Virtual Try-On: Bridging the gap between digital and physical by allowing customers to visualize jewelry in real-time.
  • 02. Intelligent Support: Deployment of AI-powered chatbots to provide instant, white-glove service to global clientele.
